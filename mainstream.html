MAINSTREAM RESPONDS

"Homo Nest Raided, Queen Bees Are Stinging Mad"

Jerry Lisker, writer for the The New York Daily News, published an article on July 6th, 1969, that painted an unfair image of the patrons of the Stonewall Inn and of the Stonewall Riots. Lisker described the scene during the riots as, "...Queen Power exploded with all the fury of a gay atomic bomb. Queens, princesses, and ladies-in-waiting began hulring anything they could get their polished, manicured fingernails on." Their weapons were "femme fatale missiles." In general, Lisker's description made the riots seem ludicrious.

However, his sentiments were echoed in other mainstream news articles at the time as well, many scolding the rioters for putting up arrest with the police and for their lifestyle. To see the article, click on the image to the left.
